How we met

David was pretty content spending his life as a free man, travelling to Thailand for Muay Thai, eating salads and protein on rotation and playing doctor data at work, putting the information world to right. The one “data” issue he hadn't cracked, was the other kind – dat-ing!

Crystal equally had the travel bug exploring the globe whenever she had the time and funds, secretly sneaking in KFC in cinemas and also putting the world to right, through her charity career. She loved arranging evenings full of fun (like pass-the-parcel!) and playing cupid for other couples, however, she also hadn't yet won one of the most important of life's entertainment: the game of love!

Luckily, for David, (disclaimer, copy was written by Crystal), on a fateful day in April 2018, David logged onto his dating app, “Happn” and went on a real splurge, forking out a whole British £1 to “super-like” a girl called Crystal.

Their first date entailed the intention of a trendy rooftop cocktail bar but without a reservation, became a trip to the local supermarket to pick up tins of prosecco/beer and sitting under the London Eye getting to know each other. When the sun went down, not wanting the evening to end, the date extended to a trip to the local amusement arcade (see left photo!). Way past midnight, Crystal knew how to clinch a second date: by pulling out two boiled eggs from her magic handbag, which they attractively wolfed down together having not eaten the whole night (crazy for two obsessed foodies!).

After that protein offering four years ago, they dated ‘Happn-ly ever after' and are so excited to now be getting hitched surrounded by the very best people in their life!

The Finer Details

The Canarian Island of Tenerife

For those that haven't been before, we chose the volcanic island for its almost guaranteed sunny weather and its reach for those travelling from the UK, France, and US (comparative to our original Bali wedding plan!).

Many will know it as a party island, but we fell in love with it for its less travelled parts (…such as Crossfit…), serene rocky mountain landscapes, good food and incredibly warm people. So, it might not look like much on arrival – a bit like Mars with hamlets of Brit ex-pat modern-builds, but we hope you'll spend a few extra days after the wedding exploring it with us :)

The Weather

In October, the weather should hover around 22 degrees in the daytime and 18 degrees at its coolest in the evenings – US folks, that's 70F and 64F. That might not sound super hot, but it's usually cloudless so it feels like a pretty perfect temperature during the day!

Our venue is in the mountains so it can be a little cool and windy in the evening so definitely bring a jacket if you're not going to be grooving on the dancefloor all night!

The Venue

Granje de Bastian – it's in a pretty remote location, so we shall be arranging transport on the wedding day to and also from the venue. We also have limited spots to stay on-venue, if you'd be interested then please let us know in the survey and we shall try our best to accommodate. This means if you need to say, store a spare of flats or jacket or anything else that can be possible! It has three pools, a tennis court, a foosball table, kitchens across three buildings and lots of parking!

The venue is about a 15 minute drive from South Tenerife Airport (TFS) and 30-minute drive from Adeje.

Wedding transport

Will be arranged to and from a central location(s) to the venue. In the evening, we'll arrange a couple of departure times for guests. If you rent a car, there is plenty of parking onsite (we are literally in a mountainous desert), or you can also take a taxi back.

What activities are planned?

Please share the type activities you'd like to do together in the survey! No one should feel obliged to hang out together, but if people would like to then we'll try and group everyone up! Also, these are all suggestions and we're open to ideas and thoughts!

We've tried to share very approximate pricing to help you make your decision!

Wine-tasting (~£30-£50/ $40-$65) – we will likely do this on the Monday while the Chiu and Szajngarten residents are in town! Possibly with mojo/cooking class with lunch and of course, more wine!

Hiking – there are some very cool hiking trails which are for all abilities. Just to give you a flavour of options:

  • Hell's Gorge (£10/$13 – 2-3 hours of easy hiking)
  • Masca hike (£14/$18) from one of the prettiest villages in Tenerife, through a protected gorge and down to a beach. You then take a boat back to the coast -easy/moderate hike
  • Mount Teide Hike – highest mountain in Spain. Lots of options - you can either do a moderate or hard hike, or go on a cable car return trip for £32 -$42. For those who would like to hike it, we will need to get permits in advance– these are free, but they go quickly! If people want to stay on Mount Teide overnight, then the starry skys and seeing the sunset is mean to be sensational (e.g staying at the Altavista refuge if it's open by then)
  • Mount Teide and Star-gazing (£48/ $63) – with dinner and a guide

Beach Club - Bahia Beach Club – we met a cool guy who owns Bahia beach Club with his brother-in-law. It's a very chill place, with amazing food where we can just relax all day on big beach beds or play volleyball. It's not got direct access to the sea as it's quite choppy, but there's some rockpools..? Lots of other beach bar options too!

Day at the Beach – There are lots of beaches all over Tenerife, but this is a volcanic island so they are not normally yellow sanded. There are plenty that have sand brought in though!

Rent a private boat/catamaran (depending on number of people - £40/$52 for 3-hours) – go swimming, see some dolphins, have a beach/boat picnic.

Go-Karting (£11/$13)– just good, clean diesel fun for a couple of hours!

Siam Waterpark (£23/$27) – Siam Park looks cool and we've never been to one together…it could be fun?! Number 1 in the world and almost, ALMOST replaces us not being able to wed in SE Asia…just a smidgen?

Spa/well-being day – there's Thai massages, spas and even a beer spa...

Crossfit – (£28/$33) Unlimited WOD/ drop-ins for the week. We've been to this one and it's great! You knew we would put a workout thing in!
